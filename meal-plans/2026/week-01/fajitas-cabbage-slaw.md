# Fajitas & Yogurt Cabbage Slaw

## Ingredients

### Filling

- 1 lb chicken breast or beef, cubed
- 0.5 tbsp avocado oil
- 1-2 bell peppers, sliced (any color)
- 1 onion, sliced (red or yellow)

### Seasoning Blend

- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 1.5 tsp Mexican oregano
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp coriander

### Cabbage Slaw

- 0.5 head cabbage, shredded (green or purple)
- 0.5 cup carrots, julienned
- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (non-fat or low-fat)
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 lime juice

### Assembly

- Low-carb or whole wheat tortillas
- Salsa (Pico de Gallo or your favorite)

**Prep Notes:**

- Slice peppers and onions to a similar thickness as the meat for even cooking.
- Make the slaw first to let the flavors meld while you cook the meat.

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Make the Slaw**:

    - In a medium bowl, mix the Greek yogurt, cumin, and lime juice.
    - Toss with the cabbage and carrots until coated.
    - Refrigerate until serving.

2.  **Mix Seasoning**: Combine all spices in a small bowl.

3.  **Cook Filling**:

    - Heat avocado oil in a large skillet or cast iron pan over medium-high heat.
    - Add chicken or beef and half the seasoning. Cook until browned and nearly cooked through (5-7 mins). Remove from pan if needed to avoid overcrowding.
    - Add onions and peppers to the hot pan. Saut√© until tender-crisp (3-5 mins).
    - Return meat to the pan. Add the remaining seasoning and splash of water if dry.
    - Cook for 1-2 minutes until everything is heated through.

4.  **Assemble**:
    - Warm tortillas in a dry skillet.
    - Fill with meat/veggie mixture.
    - Top with a generous portion of the yogurt cabbage slaw.
    - Add salsa to taste.
