# Tofu Egg Roll in a Bowl

## Ingredients

### Base

- 8 oz firm tofu, pressed and crumbled or cubed
- 0.5 tbsp avocado oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, minced

### The Crunch

- 0.5 head cabbage, shredded
- 2-3 carrots, julienned
- 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
- Green onions, sliced (for garnish)

### Sauce

- 1 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp plum sauce (for sweetness)
- Chili garlic sauce (to taste, for heat)

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Cook Tofu**:

    - Heat oil in a large skillet/wok over medium-high heat.
    - Add crumbled or cubed tofu. Cook until golden and slightly crispy (5-7 mins).
    - Add onion and mushrooms. Cook until softened (3-4 mins).

2.  **Aromatics**:

    - Add garlic and ginger. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant.

3.  **Wilt Vegetables**:

    - Add cabbage, carrots, and bell pepper. Toss to combine.
    - Cook 3-5 minutes until cabbage is wilted but still has crunch.

4.  **Sauce**:

    - Combine soy sauce, sesame oil, oyster sauce, fish sauce, plum sauce, and chili garlic sauce.
    - Pour over the mixture and toss well.
    - Cook for 1-2 minutes more.

5.  **Serve**: Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds if desired.

## Notes

- **Crispy Tofu**: For extra crispy tofu, press it well and cook longer before adding other ingredients.
