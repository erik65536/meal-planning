# White Bean & Kale Soup

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 tbsp avocado oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 0.5 cup carrots, sliced
- 2 celery stalks, sliced
- Lots of garlic (6+ cloves), minced

### Protein & Beans

- 2 x 15 oz cans white beans (Cannellini, Navy, or Great Northern), drained and rinsed

### Liquid & Seasoning

- 4-6 cups water
- 2 packets sodium-free chicken bouillon powder
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 15 oz can diced tomatoes

### Greens

- 4 cups kale, stems removed and chopped
- 1 tbsp lemon juice

**Prep Notes:**

- Mashing 1/2 cup of the beans before adding them thickens the soup naturally.
- Remove tough stems from kale before chopping.

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Saut√©**:

    - Heat oil in a soup pot over medium heat.
    - Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook 5-7 minutes until soft.
    - Add lots of garlic. Cook 2 minutes until fragrant.

2.  **Mash Some Beans**:

    - Remove about 1/2 cup of beans and mash them with a fork.
    - This will help thicken the soup.

3.  **Simmer**:

    - Add dried herbs (thyme, oregano, paprika, cumin) and bouillon to the pot.
    - Add water, all beans (including mashed ones), and diced tomatoes.
    - Bring to boil, reduce heat, and simmer 15-20 minutes.

4.  **Finish**:
    - Stir in kale. Cook 3-5 minutes until wilted.
    - Stir in lemon juice.
    - Season with pepper to taste.
