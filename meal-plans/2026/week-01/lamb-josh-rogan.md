# Lamb Josh Rogan

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 tbsp avocado oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, minced

### Spices

- 1 jar Kashmiri Curry Sauce
- 1 packet sodium-free chicken bouillon powder
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp fenugreek powder (methi)

### Sauce & Veggies

- 1 cauliflower, cut into small florets
- 1-3 carrots, sliced
- 0.5 cup frozen peas
- 2-3 cups fresh spinach

### Protein

- 1 lb pre-cooked lamb, cubed

### Serving

- 0.5 cup dry basmati rice, cooked
- [Optional] Fresh cilantro, chopped

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Sauté Base**:

    - Heat oil in a large pot/Dutch oven over medium heat.
    - Add onion and carrots. Sauté 5-7 minutes.
    - Add garlic, ginger, and all spices. Cook 1 minute until fragrant.

2.  **Build Sauce**:

    - Pour in Kashmiri Curry Sauce.
    - Stir in bouillon powder.
    - Add cauliflower.
    - Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes until cauliflower is tender.

3.  **Add Lamb & Greens**:

    - Add pre-cooked lamb cubes.
    - Simmer for 5 minutes to heat through.
    - Stir in frozen peas and fresh spinach.
    - Cook for 2-3 minutes until spinach wilts.

4.  **Finish**:

    - Remove from heat.
    - Taste and adjust seasoning.

5.  **Serve**: Over basmati rice with cilantro and naan.
