# Breaded Salmon with Smoky Roasted Green Beans & Cherry Tomatoes

Pre-seasoned, pre-breaded salmon fillets with a sheet pan of blistered cherry tomatoes and crispy-edged green beans seasoned with smoked paprika and garlic. Bold, colorful, ~395 calories.

## Ingredients

### Salmon

- 1 pre-seasoned, pre-breaded salmon fillets (275 cal total per package)
- Cooking spray or parchment paper

### Smoky Roasted Green Beans & Cherry Tomatoes

- 12 oz (about 3 cups) fresh green beans, trimmed
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, left whole
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.25 tsp onion powder
- 0.25 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt & Black Pepper
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar (drizzled after roasting)

### Finishing

- Fresh basil or flat-leaf parsley, roughly torn
- Lemon wedge

**Prep Notes:**

- Trim the stem end of green beans only; leave the tail end on.
- Cherry tomatoes will burst and release their juices in the oven — this creates a natural sauce that coats the green beans. Let it happen.
- Pat green beans dry before tossing with oil so they roast instead of steam.
- Follow package instructions for salmon; typically 375-400°F for 18-22 minutes.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Preheat**: Oven to 425°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.

2. **Season Vegetables**:
   - Toss green beans and cherry tomatoes together with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
   - Spread in a single layer on one baking sheet. Do not crowd.

3. **Roast Vegetables**:
   - Roast at 425°F for 18-20 minutes, until green beans are tender with some charred edges and tomatoes are blistered and starting to burst.

4. **Cook Salmon**:
   - Place salmon fillets on the second baking sheet.
   - Cook according to package instructions (typically 375-400°F for 18-22 minutes until golden and cooked through).

5. **Finish & Serve**:
   - Remove vegetables from oven. Drizzle balsamic vinegar over the pan and toss to coat.
   - Plate salmon alongside the green beans and tomatoes. Squeeze lemon over everything.
   - Tear fresh basil or parsley over the top.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~395 total (salmon 275 + green beans ~75 + cherry tomatoes ~45).
- **Blistered Tomatoes**: The burst cherry tomatoes create a light sauce that clings to the green beans — this is the flavor trick that makes simple vegetables taste indulgent.
- **Smoked Paprika**: Do not skip — this is the seasoning that elevates the vegetable side from plain to bold.
