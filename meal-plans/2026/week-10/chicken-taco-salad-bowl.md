# Chicken Taco Salad Bowl

Taco Tuesday as a lettuce bowl — spiced shredded chicken over a generous mound of romaine with black beans, cherry tomatoes, pickled jalapeño, and a cumin-lime Greek yogurt dressing standing in for sour cream. No chips, no tortilla, all the taco flavors. ~510 calories.

## Ingredients

### Spiced Shredded Chicken

- 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (6 oz)
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 0.25 cup yellow onion, finely diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1.5 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp dried oregano
- 0.25 tsp garlic powder
- 0.25 tsp onion powder
- Pinch of cayenne
- Salt & Black Pepper
- 0.25 cup chicken broth or water (for poaching/shredding)

### Salad Base

- 4 cups romaine lettuce, roughly chopped
- 0.33 cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed
- 0.5 cup cherry tomatoes, halved (from the week's shared cherry tomatoes)
- 0.25 cup red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
- Pickled jalapeño slices, to taste

### Cumin-Lime Yogurt Dressing

- 3 tbsp plain Greek yogurt (0%)
- 1 tbsp lime juice (about 0.5 lime)
- 0.25 tsp cumin
- 0.25 tsp garlic powder
- Pinch of salt
- 1 tbsp water (to thin if needed)

### Finishing

- 1 whole avocado, sliced
- Fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
- Extra lime wedge
- Hot sauce (Cholula, Valentina, or Tapatio)

**Prep Notes:**

- Poach or bake the chicken breast first, then shred with two forks — poaching keeps it moist and takes about 15 minutes; baking at 375°F takes 20-25 minutes.
- Season the shredded chicken in a hot skillet with the spices for 1-2 minutes — this blooms the spices and crisps the edges slightly, which a plain poached breast won't have.
- The dressing doubles as a drizzle and a dip — make it a little thicker if you prefer to drizzle; add water to thin.
- Red onion can be soaked in cold water for 5 minutes to mellow the sharpness, then drained.
- Avocado should go on last — it browns quickly once cut.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Cook & Shred Chicken**:
   - Season chicken breast with salt and pepper. Place in a small saucepan and cover with cold water or chicken broth.
   - Bring to a gentle simmer and poach 13-15 minutes until cooked through (internal temp 165°F). Remove and let cool 5 minutes.
   - Shred with two forks into bite-sized strips.
   - Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion and cook 3-4 minutes until softened. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
   - Add shredded chicken to the pan. Add all spices, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat and cook 2 minutes, letting the edges crisp slightly. Taste and adjust.

2. **Make Dressing**:
   - Whisk together Greek yogurt, lime juice, cumin, garlic powder, and salt in a small bowl.
   - Add water to reach a pourable consistency. Taste and adjust lime and salt.

3. **Assemble**:
   - Spread romaine over a large bowl or plate.
   - Spoon hot chicken over the center.
   - Arrange black beans, cherry tomatoes, and red onion around the turkey.
   - Fan avocado slices over the top.
   - Drizzle yogurt dressing over everything.
   - Top with pickled jalapeño, cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and hot sauce.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~510 total (chicken ~160 + romaine ~20 + black beans ~45 + tomatoes/onion ~20 + avocado ~240 + yogurt dressing ~25).
- **Taco Without the Tortilla**: Romaine provides crunch and bulk for very few calories. The volumetric payoff is excellent.
- **Chicken**: Poaching keeps the breast moist and neutral so the spice blend does the work. Searing in the skillet after shredding adds the texture and flavor that browning provides.
- **Black Beans**: Use leftover beans from the week's stuffed peppers — open 1 can, use 0.33 cup here and the rest in stuffed peppers.
