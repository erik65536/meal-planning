# Egg Roll in a Bowl

All the flavors of an egg roll — savory chopped pork loin, wilted cabbage, ginger, and a punchy umami sauce — without the wrapper or the deep frying. One pan, 20 minutes. ~390 calories.

## Ingredients

### Base

- 6 oz pork loin, finely chopped into small pieces
- 0.5 tbsp avocado oil
- 1 small yellow onion, diced
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch fresh ginger, minced or grated

### The Crunch

- 1 small head green cabbage, shredded
- 2 medium carrots, julienned or shredded
- 3 green onions, sliced (whites and greens separated)

### Sauce

- 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce or tamari
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp rice vinegar
- 0.5 tsp chili garlic sauce or sriracha (adjust to heat preference)

### Garnish

- 1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted
- Extra green onion tops
- Small drizzle of chili oil

**Prep Notes:**

- Chop the pork loin into very small pieces — aim for about 0.25-inch bits. Smaller pieces cook faster, brown better, and distribute through the dish the way ground meat would. Slice the loin against the grain first, then mince down from there.
- Shredding the cabbage thin is key — thick chunks don't wilt evenly and stay raw in the center. A mandoline makes this very fast; a sharp chef's knife works too.
- Mix the sauce in a small bowl before you start cooking so it's ready to pour immediately when needed.
- Pork loin is much leaner than ground pork — there won't be much rendered fat to drain, but give the pan a check anyway.
- Don't overcook the cabbage; pull it off heat while it still has a slight bite for the authentic egg roll texture.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Cook Pork**:
   - Heat avocado oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
   - Add chopped pork loin. Spread into a single layer and cook undisturbed 2 minutes until browned on the bottom. Stir and cook another 2 minutes until cooked through.
   - Add onion and mushrooms. Cook 4-5 minutes until onion is softened and mushrooms have released their liquid and started to brown.

2. **Aromatics**:
   - Push the mixture to the side. Add garlic, ginger, and green onion whites to the center of the pan.
   - Cook 30-60 seconds until fragrant, then stir into the pork mixture.

3. **Wilt Cabbage**:
   - Add shredded cabbage and carrots to the pan. Toss everything together.
   - Cook 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cabbage is wilted but still has a little crunch.

4. **Sauce**:
   - Pour the combined sauce (soy sauce, sesame oil, oyster sauce, fish sauce, rice vinegar, chili garlic sauce) over the mixture.
   - Toss well to coat everything evenly. Cook 1 more minute.

5. **Serve**:
   - Transfer to a bowl. Top with green onion tops, sesame seeds, and a drizzle of chili oil.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~390 total (pork loin ~185 + cabbage and carrots ~65 + mushrooms ~25 + sauce ~35 + oil ~25 + sesame/chili oil ~55) — pork loin is significantly leaner than ground pork.
- This reheats well — great for lunch the next day; the cabbage softens further overnight.
