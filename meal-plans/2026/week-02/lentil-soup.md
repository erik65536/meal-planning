# Lentil Soup (Curry + Kale)

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced

### The Lentils

- 1 cup dry lentils (Green or Brown hold shape best; Red break down for creamier soup)
- 4 cups vegetable broth (or water + bouillon), plus more as needed
- 1 x 14-15 oz can diced tomatoes
- 1 x 14-15 oz can crushed tomatoes

### Seasoning

- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 tsp curry powder

### Finish

- 2 cups kale, chopped (stems removed)
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Salt + pepper, to taste

### To Serve

- Greek yogurt (a dollop on each bowl)
- Fresh parsley or cilantro (optional)

**Prep Notes:**

- Pick through and rinse lentils; no soaking needed.
- If the soup gets too thick, add a splash of broth/water near the end.

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Saut√©**:

    - Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
    - Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook 5-7 minutes until softened.
    - Add garlic, cumin, coriander, thyme, curry powder, and red pepper flakes (if using). Cook 1 minute.

2.  **Simmer**:

    - Add lentils, broth, diced tomatoes, crushed tomatoes, and bay leaf.
    - Bring to a boil, then reduce to a gentle simmer.
    - Simmer partially covered for 25-35 minutes, until lentils are tender.

3.  **Finish**:

    - Remove bay leaf.
    - Stir in kale and cook 3-5 minutes until wilted.
    - Stir in lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.

4.  **Serve**:

    - Ladle into bowls and top with a dollop of Greek yogurt.
