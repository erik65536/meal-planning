# Black Bean Soup

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 tbsp avocado oil (or olive oil)
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1-2 carrots, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced

### Beans & Broth

- 2 x 15 oz cans black beans
  - Drain and rinse 1 can; keep the liquid in the 2nd can (helps thicken)
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 1 x 14-15 oz can diced tomatoes

### Spices

- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp oregano
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

### Finish

- 1-2 tbsp lime juice (to taste)

### Toppings

- Greek yogurt
- Avocado
- Lime wedges

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Saut√©**:

    - Heat oil in a pot over medium heat.
    - Add onion, bell pepper, celery, and carrots. Cook 5-8 minutes until softened.
    - Add garlic and spices. Cook 1 minute.

2.  **Simmer**:

    - Add black beans (including the liquid from 1 can), broth, and diced tomatoes.
    - Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer. Cook 15 minutes.

3.  **Blend**:

    - Blend about half the soup (immersion blender or transfer ~2 cups to a blender), leaving some whole beans.

4.  **Finish**:

    - Stir in lime juice and adjust salt/pepper.
    - Serve hot with Greek yogurt, avocado, and lime wedges.
