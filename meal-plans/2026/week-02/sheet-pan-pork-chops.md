# Sheet Pan Cajun Pork Chops + Veggies (Yogurt Sauce)

## Ingredients

### Protein

- 2 bone-in pork chops
- 1-2 tbsp Cajun seasoning (to taste)
- 1 tbsp olive oil (or avocado oil)
- Salt & pepper (optional; Cajun blends vary)

### Veggies

- 1 large sweet potato, cut into 0.5-inch cubes
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 red onion, sliced
- 8 oz green beans (trimmed)

### Greek Yogurt Topping (For Veggies)

- 0.75-1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 garlic cloves, grated/minced
- 0.5 tsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped (or 1 tsp dried dill)
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Salt & pepper, to taste

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Preheat**: Oven to 425°F.

2.  **Season Veggies**:

    - Toss sweet potato, bell pepper, red onion, and green beans with oil and Cajun seasoning.
    - Spread on a sheet pan in a single layer.

3.  **Roast Veggies (Start First)**:

    - Roast 15 minutes.

4.  **Season + Add Pork Chops**:

    - Pat pork chops dry.
    - Rub with a little oil and Cajun seasoning.
    - Remove sheet pan, push veggies to the sides, and place chops in the center.

5.  **Finish Roasting**:

    - Roast 12-18 minutes more, until pork reaches 145°F.
    - Rest pork chops 5 minutes.

6.  **Make Yogurt Topping**:

    - Stir together Greek yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, onion powder, dill, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

7.  **Serve**:

    - Serve pork chops with roasted veggies.
    - Spoon the Greek yogurt topping over the veggies (or serve on the side).
