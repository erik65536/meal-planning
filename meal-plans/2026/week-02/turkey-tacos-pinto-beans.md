# Ground Turkey Tacos + Crusted Pinto Beans (Roasted Red Peppers)

## Ingredients

### Turkey Filling

- 1 lb ground turkey
- 0.5-1 tbsp avocado oil (or olive oil)
- 1 onion, sliced or diced

### Turkey Seasoning

- 2 tsp cumin
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp onion powder
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

### Crusted Pinto Beans

- 2 x 15 oz cans pinto beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp cornstarch (helps make a crisp “crust”)
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp onion powder
- 0.5 tsp dried oregano
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

### To Serve

- Low carb tortillas
- 1 x 12-16 oz jar roasted red peppers, drained and sliced (topping)

### Side: Roasted Cabbage Steaks

- 1 head green cabbage
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- Salt & black pepper, to taste
- Lime wedges (for serving)

### Yogurt Sauce (For Cabbage)

- 0.5 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 small garlic clove, grated/minced
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

## Cooking Instructions

0.  **Roast Cabbage Steaks (Side)**:

    - Heat oven to 425°F.
    - Slice cabbage into 0.75-1 inch “steaks” (keep the core so they hold together).
    - Place on a sheet pan, brush with oil, and season with chili powder, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    - Roast 20-25 minutes, flipping once, until browned on the edges.
    - Stir together Greek yogurt, lime juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.
    - Serve cabbage with a squeeze of lime and a spoonful of yogurt sauce.

1.  **Crisp the Pinto Beans (Skillet)**:

    - Pat drained beans dry with paper towels.
    - Toss with oil, cornstarch, and all bean seasonings.

    - Cook in a large skillet over medium-high heat for 8-12 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned and lightly crisp.

2.  **Cook Turkey**:

    - Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
    - Add onion and cook 3-4 minutes until softened.
    - Add ground turkey and cook until browned.
    - Stir in the turkey seasoning and a splash of water if the pan looks dry.

3.  **Assemble Tacos**:

    - Warm tortillas.
    - Fill with turkey and top with crusted pinto beans.
    - Top with roasted red peppers.
