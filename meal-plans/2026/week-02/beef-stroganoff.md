# Beef Stroganoff (Greek Yogurt + Spinach)

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 tbsp olive oil or avocado oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 16 oz mushrooms, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced

### Beef (Pick One)

- 1 lb beef strips (any quick-cooking beef), OR
- 1 lb shaved beef, OR
- 1 lb beef stew meat (small pieces)

### The Sauce

- 2 cups beef broth (or low-sodium)
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 0.5 tsp soy sauce powder
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water (slurry)
- Salt + black pepper, to taste

### Finish

- 4 cups spinach, chopped
- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt

### To Serve

- Egg noodles
- Fresh parsley (optional)

**Prep Notes:**

- Bring the Greek yogurt closer to room temp to reduce curdling.

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Cook Beef**:

    - Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    - Add beef and season with salt/pepper.
    - Cook until browned (ground beef: fully cooked; strips/shaved/stew pieces: browned outside). Remove to a plate.

2.  **Cook Veggies**:

    - Add mushrooms to the same pan. Cook 4-6 minutes until browned.
    - Add onion and cook 3-4 minutes until softened.
    - Add garlic and thyme; cook 1 minute.

3.  **Make Sauce**:

    - Add broth, Dijon, Worcestershire, and soy sauce powder. Stir to combine.
    - Return beef to the pan and simmer 3-5 minutes.
    - Stir in cornstarch slurry and simmer 1-2 minutes until thickened.

4.  **Finish**:

    - Remove from heat.
    - Stir in spinach until wilted.
    - Stir in Greek yogurt until creamy (do not boil after adding).

5.  **Serve**:

    - Serve over noodles. Top with parsley if desired.
