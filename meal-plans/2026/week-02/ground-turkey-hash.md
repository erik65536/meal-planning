# Ground Turkey Hash (Sweet Potato + Kale + Eggs)

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 tbsp avocado oil (or olive oil), plus more as needed
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced small (about 1/2-inch cubes)

### Protein & Greens

- 1 lb ground turkey
- 4 cups kale, stems removed and chopped
- 4 eggs

### Seasoning

- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 0.25 tsp turmeric
- Salt & black pepper, to taste

## Cooking Instructions

1.  **Cook Turkey**:

    - Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    - Add ground turkey, season with salt/pepper, and cook until browned.
    - Transfer turkey to a plate.

2.  **Crisp Sweet Potatoes**:

    - Add a little more oil if the pan is dry.
    - Add onion and sweet potatoes.
    - Spread into an even layer and cook mostly undisturbed 3-4 minutes to brown.
    - Stir and repeat until sweet potatoes are tender and crispy, 10-15 minutes.
    - Stir in smoked paprika, thyme, garlic powder, onion powder, curry powder, and turmeric.

3.  **Add Kale + Turkey**:

    - Stir in kale and cook 2-3 minutes until wilted.
    - Return turkey to the skillet and toss to combine.

4.  **Add Eggs**:

    - Make 4 small wells in the hash.
    - Crack 1 egg into each well.
    - Cover and cook on low until whites are set, 5-7 minutes.

5.  **Serve**: Season to taste and serve hot.
