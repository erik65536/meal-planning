# Pork & Kimchi Stir Fry with Rice

## Ingredients

### Base

- 1 lb pork loin, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1 tbsp avocado oil or vegetable oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch fresh ginger, minced
- 1 bag (12-16 oz) frozen stir-fry veggie mix
- 1 cup radish kimchi, roughly chopped

### Rice

- 1 cup jasmine rice or short grain white rice
- 2 cups water
- Pinch of salt

### Korean-Style Sauce

- 2 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp fish sauce
- 2 tsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
- 1 tsp honey or brown sugar
- 1 tsp gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes) - optional for extra heat

### For Serving

- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds
- Fried egg on top

**Prep Notes:**

- Cut pork loin into uniform 1-inch cubes for even cooking
- Mix sauce ingredients before starting to cook
- Kimchi adds probiotics and tangy, spicy flavor
- Frozen veggie mix keeps this weeknight-friendly

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Cook Rice**:
   - Rinse rice until water runs clear
   - Combine rice, water, and salt in pot
   - Bring to boil over high heat
   - Immediately reduce to lowest heat, cover tightly
   - Cook 15 minutes without lifting lid
   - Remove from heat, let rest covered 5-10 minutes
   - Fluff with fork

2. **Prepare Sauce**:
   - In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, fish sauce, rice vinegar, gochujang, honey, and gochugaru
   - Set aside

3. **Cook Pork**:
   - Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat
   - Add pork cubes in single layer (work in batches if needed to avoid crowding)
   - Sear 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through
   - Remove pork to a plate and set aside

4. **Cook Vegetables**:
   - Add a bit more oil to pan if needed
   - Add sliced onion and frozen veggie mix
   - Stir-fry 4-5 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp and heated through
   - Add garlic and ginger
   - Cook 30 seconds until fragrant, stirring constantly

5. **Combine**:
   - Return pork to pan
   - Add chopped kimchi
   - Pour sauce over everything
   - Toss vigorously for 1-2 minutes until everything is coated and heated through

6. **Serve**:
   - Serve stir-fry over rice
   - Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds
   - Top with fried egg if desired

## Notes

- Pork loin is lean and cooks quickly - don't overcook or it will dry out
- Kimchi provides probiotics and authentic Korean flavor
- Frozen veggie mix saves prep time
- Gochujang adds savory, spicy, umami depth
- High heat is key for good stir-fry - don't overcrowd the pan
- Fried egg on top adds richness and makes it feel like Korean bibimbap
