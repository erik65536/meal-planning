# Chicken Taco Salad

Shredded taco-spiced chicken over a massive bed of greens with salsa, pickled jalapeños, and radishes. High volume, high protein, very low calorie. ~345 calories.

## Ingredients

### Taco Chicken

- 1 boneless skinless chicken breast (6 oz), ~185 cal
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 1.5 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp onion powder
- 0.5 tsp dried oregano
- 0.25 tsp cayenne pepper
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### Salad Base

- 4 cups mixed greens or romaine (from bag), ~15 cal
- 0.5 cup salsa (store-bought), ~25 cal
- 4 radishes, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp frozen corn, thawed or reheated
- Pickled jalapeño slices, to taste

### Finishing

- Fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
- Hot sauce (Cholula or Valentina)
- Extra lime wedge

**Prep Notes:**

- Shredded chicken works better than sliced here — it soaks up the taco spices in the pan and catches the dressing and salsa. To shred: cook until 165°F, then pull apart with two forks while still warm.
- Pound the chicken breast to an even thickness before cooking if one end is significantly thicker — this prevents the thin end from drying out while the thick end finishes cooking.
- Salsa serves as the primary dressing — no separate dressing needed.
- Build the salad just before eating — don't dress ahead or the greens will wilt under the warm chicken.
- Radishes add crunch and volume for almost no calories; load them up.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Cook Chicken**:
   - Pat chicken breast dry. Coat with avocado oil, then press the taco spice blend (cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, cayenne, salt, pepper) into both sides.
   - Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken 6-7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (165°F internal). Let rest 5 minutes.
   - Shred the chicken using two forks. Return shredded chicken to the pan briefly over medium heat, stirring to pick up any remaining spiced fond in the pan. Remove from heat.

2. **Assemble**:
   - Spread greens in a large bowl or on a wide plate.
   - Spoon salsa over the greens. Add radishes, corn, and pickled jalapeños.
   - Pile warm shredded chicken over the top.
   - Top with fresh cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and hot sauce.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~360 total (chicken ~185 + greens ~15 + salsa ~25 + radishes ~10 + corn ~15 + oil ~40 + misc ~15).
- Meal prep option: cook and shred the chicken in advance, store in the fridge, and reheat in a dry pan before assembling the salad.
