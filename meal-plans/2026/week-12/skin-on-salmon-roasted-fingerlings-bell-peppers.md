# Skin-On Salmon with Roasted Fingerling Potatoes and Bell Peppers

Skin-on salmon filet with a smoky paprika-coriander crust roasted alongside crispy fingerling potatoes and sweet caramelized bell peppers. ~730 calories.

## Ingredients

### Salmon

- 1 skin-on salmon filet (about 6-7 oz), ~460 cal
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp ground coriander
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.25 tsp ground cumin
- 0.25 tsp black pepper
- 0.25 tsp salt

### Roasted Fingerlings & Bell Peppers

- 6 oz fingerling potatoes, halved lengthwise, ~150 cal
- 2 bell peppers (any colors), seeded and cut into 1-inch strips
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp cumin
- 0.5 tsp onion powder
- 0.25 tsp cayenne pepper
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### Finishing

- Fresh cilantro or flat-leaf parsley, roughly chopped
- 1 tsp sherry vinegar or red wine vinegar, drizzled over vegetables

**Prep Notes:**

- Score the skin of the salmon 2-3 times with a sharp knife — this prevents curling during roasting and helps the seasoning penetrate along the edges.
- Pat the salmon filet completely dry before applying the spice rub — moisture prevents the rub from adhering and creates steam rather than a crust.
- Rub the spice blend over the flesh side; leave the skin lightly oiled only.
- Bell peppers release a lot of liquid as they cook — spread them in a single layer with space between pieces so they caramelize rather than steam.
- Fingerlings take longer than peppers, so stagger the timing: potatoes go in first, peppers added partway through.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Preheat**: Oven to 425°F. Line a baking sheet (or two) with parchment.

2. **Season Potatoes**:
   - Toss halved fingerlings with 0.5 tbsp olive oil, garlic, smoked paprika, cumin, onion powder, cayenne, salt, and pepper. Spread cut-side down on the baking sheet.
   - Roast 12 minutes.

3. **Add Bell Peppers**:
   - Toss bell pepper strips with remaining 0.5 tbsp olive oil, a pinch of salt and pepper.
   - Add to the baking sheet with the potatoes. Roast another 10 minutes.

4. **Add Salmon**:
   - While vegetables roast, pat salmon dry and rub flesh side with avocado oil and the spice blend (smoked paprika, coriander, garlic powder, cumin, salt, pepper).
   - Place skin-side down on the baking sheet or a separate sheet.
   - Roast alongside vegetables for 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily and the flesh is opaque throughout. Thinner fillets may need only 10-12 minutes.

5. **Finish & Serve**:
   - Drizzle sherry vinegar over the roasted peppers and potatoes; toss lightly.
   - Plate salmon skin-side down alongside the roasted vegetables. Top with fresh cilantro or parsley.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~730 total (salmon ~460 + potatoes ~150 + bell peppers ~60 + oil ~60).
- The skin should pull cleanly away from the flesh once fully cooked — slide a spatula between skin and flesh to serve skinless if preferred, or serve with skin on for extra crispiness.
