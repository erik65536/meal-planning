# Breaded Salmon with Roasted Fingerling Potatoes and Cherry Tomatoes

## Ingredients

### Salmon

- 1 pre-seasoned and breaded salmon filet (from frozen or fresh), ~285 cal

### Roasted Fingerlings & Tomatoes

- 6 oz fingerling potatoes, halved lengthwise, ~150 cal
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, whole
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.25 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### Finishing

- Fresh basil or parsley, roughly torn
- 1 tsp balsamic glaze, drizzled at the end

**Prep Notes:**

- Halve fingerlings lengthwise so they lay flat — this maximizes the cut surface against the hot pan for better browning and crispiness.
- Cherry tomatoes go in whole; they'll burst and become jammy during roasting, creating a natural sauce that pools around the potatoes.
- Check the salmon packaging for specific roasting instructions — follow the package temperature if it differs from 400°F.
- Give the potatoes a 10-minute head start before adding the salmon so everything finishes at the same time.
- Don't crowd the pan — spread potatoes in a single layer cut-side down. Use a second sheet if needed.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Preheat**: Oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment.

2. **Season Potatoes**:
   - Toss halved fingerlings with olive oil, garlic slices, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, smoked paprika, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
   - Spread cut-side down on the baking sheet. Roast 10 minutes.

3. **Add Tomatoes & Salmon**:
   - After 10 minutes, nestle cherry tomatoes around the potatoes.
   - Place the breaded salmon filet on the same pan (or a separate sheet if space is tight).
   - Roast another 18-22 minutes until potatoes are golden and tender, tomatoes have burst, and salmon is cooked through and the breading is crispy.

4. **Finish & Serve**:
   - Drizzle balsamic glaze over the tomatoes and potatoes. Top with torn fresh basil or parsley.
   - Plate salmon alongside the roasted vegetables.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~520 total (salmon ~285 + potatoes ~150 + tomatoes ~20 + oil ~60 + balsamic glaze ~10).
- Timing will vary slightly depending on the thickness and brand of the breaded salmon — check the package directions.
