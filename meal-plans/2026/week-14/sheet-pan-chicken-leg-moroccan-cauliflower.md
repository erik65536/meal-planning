# Sheet Pan Chicken Leg Quarter with Moroccan Cauliflower and Carrots

Bone-in chicken leg quarter roasted over cauliflower and carrots in a warm Moroccan spice blend — cumin, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, and ginger. Finished with lemon juice and fresh cilantro. ~565 calories.

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 1 bone-in, skin-on chicken leg quarter (~12 oz)
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 0.75 tsp cumin
- 0.5 tsp turmeric
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp coriander
- 0.25 tsp cinnamon
- 0.25 tsp ginger powder
- 0.25 tsp black pepper
- 0.25 tsp cayenne
- Salt to taste

### Vegetables

- 2 cups cauliflower florets (~6 oz), broken into medium pieces
- 2 medium carrots, peeled and cut into 2-inch sticks (~0.25 inch thick)
- 1.5 tsp avocado oil
- 0.75 tsp cumin
- 0.5 tsp turmeric
- 0.5 tsp coriander
- 0.25 tsp cinnamon
- 0.25 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### Finishing

- Juice of 0.5 lemon
- 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, roughly chopped

**Prep Notes:**

- Bone-in, skin-on chicken leg quarters are two-part pieces (drumstick + thigh joined). At 425°F they take 35-40 minutes — the high heat crisps the skin while dark meat stays juicy.
- Score the skin in 2-3 places with a knife and push the spice rub under the skin directly against the meat. Flavor applied only to the outside of the skin mostly stays on the skin and doesn't reach the meat.
- Carrots and cauliflower go in at the same time — slice carrots thin enough (~0.25 inch) that they roast in the same window as the cauliflower.
- Use a rimmed baking sheet or roasting pan — the chicken releases a significant amount of fat and juice during roasting. Flat sheets can spill.
- The cinnamon is traditional in North African spice blends (ras el hanout and similar) — it reads as warmth, not sweetness, and is the backbone of the profile. Don't omit it.
- Turmeric stains everything yellow. Use a lined pan.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Preheat**: Oven to 425°F. Line a large rimmed baking sheet with foil or parchment.

2. **Prepare Chicken**:
   - Mix all chicken spices. Score the skin in 2-3 shallow slits with a knife.
   - Rub the chicken with avocado oil, then push the spice blend under the skin. Rub the rest all over the outside. Season generously with salt.

3. **Prepare Vegetables**:
   - Toss cauliflower florets and carrot sticks with avocado oil, cumin, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
   - Spread in a single layer on the baking sheet, leaving space in the center or to the side for the chicken.

4. **Roast**:
   - Place the seasoned chicken leg quarter on top of or alongside the vegetables.
   - Roast at 425°F for 35-40 minutes until chicken skin is deep golden and crispy and the thickest part of the thigh (not touching bone) reaches 165°F.

5. **Finish and Serve**:
   - Rest chicken 5 minutes.
   - Squeeze lemon juice over everything. Scatter fresh cilantro.
   - Spoon the roasting pan juices over the chicken and vegetables before plating.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~565 total (chicken ~350 + cauliflower ~40 + carrots ~50 + oil ~120 + lemon ~5).
- The roasting pan juices pick up the spices and chicken fat — they act as a natural sauce. Don't skip spooning them over the plate.
- Cauliflower is done when the florets have deep golden spots and the edges are starting to char. Pale cauliflower tastes plain; caramelized cauliflower is nutty and sweet.
