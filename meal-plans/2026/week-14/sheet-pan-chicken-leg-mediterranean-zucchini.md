# Sheet Pan Chicken Leg Quarter with Mediterranean Zucchini and Tomatoes

Bone-in chicken leg quarter roasted over zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and garlic with oregano and lemon. Bright, aromatic, and built on the pan juices. ~480 calories.

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 1 bone-in, skin-on chicken leg quarter (~12 oz)
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 0.75 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.25 tsp onion powder
- 0.25 tsp dried thyme
- 0.25 tsp black pepper
- Salt to taste

### Vegetables

- 1 medium zucchini (~8 oz), cut into 0.5-inch half-moons
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes (~6 oz), left whole
- 3 cloves garlic, smashed
- 1.5 tsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.25 tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### Finishing

- Juice of 0.5 lemon + zest of 0.5 lemon
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley or basil, roughly chopped

**Prep Notes:**

- Bone-in, skin-on chicken leg quarters roast best at 425°F for 35-40 minutes — the high heat crisps the skin while dark meat stays juicy. Score the skin and push the spice rub underneath to season the meat directly.
- Cherry tomatoes go in during the last 15-18 minutes only — they cook fast and become bitter if they roast for the full duration. Add them when you check the midpoint.
- Zucchini releases water as it roasts — spread it out and don't crowd it. A crowded pan steams instead of roasts.
- The cherry tomatoes burst and mix with the olive oil and chicken drippings into a natural pan sauce. Spoon it generously over everything when plating.
- Zest the lemon before cutting and juicing it. Add the zest at the end, not during roasting — heat makes citrus zest bitter.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Preheat**: Oven to 425°F. Line a large rimmed baking sheet with parchment.

2. **Prepare Chicken**:
   - Mix oregano, garlic powder, smoked paprika, onion powder, thyme, and black pepper. Score the skin in 2-3 shallow slits.
   - Rub the chicken with avocado oil, then push the spice blend under the skin. Rub the remainder over the outside. Season with salt.

3. **Prepare Vegetables (except tomatoes)**:
   - Toss zucchini half-moons and smashed garlic with olive oil, oregano, smoked paprika, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
   - Spread in a single layer on the baking sheet. Place chicken leg quarter on top or alongside.

4. **Roast — First Half**:
   - Roast at 425°F for 20-22 minutes.

5. **Add Tomatoes and Finish Roasting**:
   - Add cherry tomatoes to the pan.
   - Continue roasting 15-18 more minutes until chicken skin is deep golden and crispy and the thickest part of the thigh (not touching bone) reaches 165°F.

6. **Rest and Finish**:
   - Rest chicken 5 minutes.
   - Squeeze lemon juice over the vegetables and zest the lemon over everything.
   - Scatter fresh parsley or basil. Spoon the pan juices over the plate before serving.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~480 total (chicken ~350 + zucchini ~35 + cherry tomatoes ~30 + garlic ~5 + olive oil ~55 + lemon ~5).
- The burst cherry tomatoes and olive oil form a loose, bright sauce in the bottom of the pan — it's the best part of the dish. Don't leave it behind.
- This is a lighter, brighter dish than the Moroccan chicken (meal 4) — the lemon-oregano profile is fresh and clean rather than warm and spiced. Save one for a lighter night.
