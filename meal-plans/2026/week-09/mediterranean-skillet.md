# Mediterranean Skillet

One-pan chicken with zucchini, cherry tomatoes, spinach, and black olives in a bright lemon-garlic-herb sauce. Bold Mediterranean flavors with minimal cleanup. Uses up the remaining black olives from the wraps earlier in the week.

## Ingredients

### Protein

- 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (6-8 oz), sliced into 0.5-inch strips or pounded thin
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- Salt & Black Pepper
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder

### Vegetables & Sauce

- 1 medium zucchini, halved lengthwise and sliced into half moons
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 3 cups baby spinach (packed)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 0.25 cup black olives, sliced (use remaining from wrap night)
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 0.5 tsp dried thyme
- 0.25 tsp red pepper flakes
- Juice of 0.5 lemon
- Zest of 0.5 lemon
- Salt & Black Pepper
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley or basil, chopped (for finishing)

**Prep Notes:**

- Pound the chicken breast to an even thickness (about 0.5 inch) or slice it thin — this ensures quick, even cooking.
- Cut zucchini half moons on the thicker side (0.5 inch) so they hold some texture after cooking.
- Add spinach at the very end — it wilts in about 60 seconds.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Sear the Chicken**:
   - Season chicken strips with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
   - Heat avocado oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
   - Add chicken in a single layer (don't crowd). Sear 3-4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (165°F internal).
   - Remove to a plate and tent loosely with foil.

2. **Cook Zucchini**:
   - In the same skillet over medium-high heat, add olive oil.
   - Add zucchini half moons. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of oregano.
   - Cook 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden on the edges.

3. **Add Aromatics & Tomatoes**:
   - Add minced garlic, remaining oregano, thyme, and red pepper flakes to the zucchini.
   - Cook 60 seconds until fragrant.
   - Add cherry tomatoes and black olives. Toss to combine.
   - Cook 2-3 minutes until tomatoes begin to soften and release juice.

4. **Wilt Spinach & Finish**:
   - Add baby spinach to the pan and toss. Cook 60-90 seconds until just wilted.
   - Squeeze lemon juice over the pan and add lemon zest.
   - Return chicken to the skillet and toss everything together to coat in the pan juices.
   - Taste and adjust seasoning.

5. **Serve**:
   - Transfer to a plate or serve from the skillet.
   - Top with fresh parsley or basil.

## Notes

- **Portion**: Single serving — 1 chicken breast with generous vegetables.
- **Calories**: ~340. Spinach and zucchini are extremely low calorie — you can pile them on without hesitation.
- **Black Olives**: Use up whatever remains from the Mediterranean Chicken Wraps. Salty, briny, and full of healthy fats.
- **No Carb Side**: This meal doesn't need anything on the side — the volume of vegetables is the bulk. Resist the urge to add rice or bread.
- **Pan Sauce**: The combination of tomato juice, lemon, and olive oil creates a light, bright sauce on its own — no need for additional liquid.
