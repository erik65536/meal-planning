# Mediterranean Chicken & Black Olive Wraps

Fresh, crisp chicken wraps with bold Mediterranean seasoning, crunchy vegetables, briny black olives, and a lemon-herb Greek yogurt sauce. Satisfying without being heavy.

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (6-8 oz), sliced into thin strips
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 0.75 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 tsp dried oregano
- 0.25 tsp onion powder
- Salt & Black Pepper

### Wraps

- 2-3 low-carb tortillas

### Wrap Fillings

- 2 cups romaine lettuce or baby spinach, roughly chopped
- 0.5 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 0.5 medium cucumber, thinly sliced into rounds
- 0.25 cup black olives, sliced or halved
- 0.25 red onion, very thinly sliced (optional)

### Lemon-Herb Greek Yogurt Sauce

- 3 tbsp Greek yogurt
- Juice of 0.5 lemon
- 1 clove garlic, minced or grated
- 0.5 tsp dried dill (or 1 tsp fresh dill)
- 0.25 tsp dried oregano
- Salt & Black Pepper

**Prep Notes:**

- Slice chicken thin so strips cook quickly and evenly — about 0.25 inch thick.
- Make the yogurt sauce first so it has a few minutes to meld while chicken cooks.
- Slice cucumber just before assembling so it stays crisp.
- Black olives are briny and salty — taste before adding extra salt.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Make Yogurt Sauce**:
   - In a small bowl, combine Greek yogurt, lemon juice, minced garlic, dill, oregano, salt, and pepper.
   - Stir well. Taste and adjust seasoning.
   - Set aside — sauce improves as it sits.

2. **Cook Chicken**:
   - Toss sliced chicken strips with avocado oil, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, oregano, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Coat well.
   - Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
   - Add chicken strips in a single layer. Cook 3-4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through (165°F internal).
   - Remove from heat and let rest 2 minutes.

3. **Warm Tortillas**:
   - Heat tortillas one at a time in the same dry skillet for 30-60 seconds per side until warm and slightly toasty.

4. **Assemble Wraps**:
   - Spread a generous spoonful of yogurt sauce down the center of each tortilla.
   - Layer romaine (or spinach), cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and black olives.
   - Top with a portion of chicken strips.
   - Drizzle a little extra yogurt sauce over the chicken.
   - Fold or roll like a burrito and serve immediately.

## Notes

- **Portion**: Makes 2-3 wraps — single serving dinner.
- **Calories**: ~360 (2 wraps; varies by tortilla brand — check label).
- **Olives**: Black olives add healthy fats and great Mediterranean flavor. The brininess elevates the whole wrap.
- **Low-Carb Tortillas**: Check the package — most run 60-100 calories each, significantly lower than standard flour tortillas.
- **Leftover Sauce**: Extra yogurt sauce keeps in the fridge for 3-4 days. Great as a vegetable dip.
