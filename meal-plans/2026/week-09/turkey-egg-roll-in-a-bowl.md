# Turkey Egg Roll in a Bowl

All the bold, savory flavors of an egg roll — none of the wrapper. Ground turkey stir-fried with shredded cabbage, carrots, garlic, and ginger in a punchy soy-sesame sauce. One pan, 20 minutes, extremely low calorie.

## Ingredients

### Protein

- 0.5 lb (8 oz) lean ground turkey

### Vegetables

- 3 cups green cabbage, thinly shredded (about 0.25 small head)
- 2 medium carrots, peeled and julienned or grated
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch fresh ginger, grated (or 0.5 tsp ground ginger)
- 3 scallions, thinly sliced (whites and greens separated)

### Sauce

- 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce or coconut aminos
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil (added at the end)
- 0.5 tsp chili garlic sauce or sriracha (adjust to taste)
- 0.5 tsp fish sauce (optional, adds depth)

### For Serving

- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- Scallion greens (reserved from above)
- [Optional] Extra sriracha or chili garlic sauce
- [Optional] Lime wedge

**Prep Notes:**

- Use a box grater for the carrots — faster than julienning and gives great texture.
- Shred cabbage thin so it softens quickly in the hot pan.
- Separate scallion whites (cook with the vegetables) from greens (garnish raw at the end).
- Ground turkey is lean; add oil to the pan to prevent sticking and keep the turkey moist.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Brown the Turkey**:
   - Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add 1 tsp avocado oil.
   - Add ground turkey. Break apart with a spatula and cook, undisturbed, for 2-3 minutes to get some browning.
   - Continue breaking apart and stirring until no pink remains, about 5-6 minutes total.
   - Push turkey to one side of the pan.

2. **Add Aromatics**:
   - Add scallion whites, garlic, and ginger to the empty side of the pan.
   - Cook 60 seconds until fragrant, then stir together with the turkey.

3. **Add Vegetables**:
   - Add shredded cabbage and grated carrots to the pan.
   - Toss everything together and stir-fry 4-5 minutes until cabbage is wilted but still has some texture — don't cook it to mush.

4. **Add Sauce**:
   - In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, rice vinegar, and chili garlic sauce.
   - Pour sauce over the pan and toss to coat everything evenly.
   - Cook 1-2 minutes more, letting the sauce absorb and reduce slightly.

5. **Finish & Serve**:
   - Remove from heat.
   - Drizzle sesame oil over the pan and toss.
   - Transfer to a bowl and top with sesame seeds and scallion greens.
   - Add a squeeze of lime and extra hot sauce if desired.

## Notes

- **Portion**: Single serving — scales easily; save any extra for lunch.
- **Calories**: ~320. Cabbage is an extraordinarily low-calorie high-volume vegetable.
- **Turkey**: 0.5 lb of lean ground turkey is roughly 150-160 calories — high protein, very lean.
- **Sesame Oil**: Add at the end off heat; it's a finishing oil and will lose its distinctive flavor if cooked.
- **Leftovers**: This reheats very well. Make the full recipe and save half for lunch the next day to cut down on cooking.
