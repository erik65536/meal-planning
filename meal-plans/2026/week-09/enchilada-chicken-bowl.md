# Enchilada Chicken Bowl with Cauliflower Rice

Shredded chicken simmered directly in enchilada sauce, served over riced cauliflower. All the flavor of enchiladas without the tortilla assembly — and without the extra calories. Using the enchilada sauce as a bold braising liquid is the key move here.

## Ingredients

### Chicken

- 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (6-8 oz)
- 1 cup red enchilada sauce (from can)
- 0.5 tsp cumin
- 0.5 tsp chili powder
- 0.25 tsp smoked paprika
- Salt & Black Pepper

### Base

- 2 cups frozen cauliflower rice (or 0.5 small head cauliflower, riced)
- 0.5 tsp avocado oil
- Salt & Black Pepper
- 0.25 tsp cumin

### Toppings

- 2 tbsp Greek yogurt (in place of sour cream)
- 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
- 0.25 cup black beans (from can, rinsed and drained) — optional for fiber
- Juice of 0.25 lime
- [Optional] 2 tbsp salsa or pico de gallo
- [Optional] 0.25 avocado, sliced

**Prep Notes:**

- The chicken simmers in the enchilada sauce until it shreds easily — this only takes about 15-18 minutes for a single breast.
- Use a small saucepan so the sauce covers most of the chicken.
- Cauliflower rice cooks in 5-6 minutes in a skillet — add it toward the end while the chicken finishes.
- If the enchilada sauce is salty, skip adding extra salt.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Simmer Chicken in Enchilada Sauce**:
   - Place chicken breast in a small saucepan. Pour enchilada sauce over top.
   - Add cumin, chili powder, and smoked paprika. Stir to combine.
   - Bring to a gentle simmer over medium heat.
   - Cover and cook 15-18 minutes, flipping once halfway, until chicken is cooked through (165°F internal).

2. **Shred Chicken**:
   - Remove chicken from sauce and shred with two forks.
   - Return shredded chicken to the pan and stir to coat in the sauce.
   - Let simmer uncovered 3-4 minutes to reduce and thicken slightly.
   - Taste and adjust seasoning.

3. **Cook Cauliflower Rice**:
   - Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
   - Add frozen cauliflower rice (no need to thaw).
   - Season with salt, pepper, and cumin.
   - Cook, stirring occasionally, 5-6 minutes until heated through and any moisture evaporates. You want some lightly golden spots.

4. **Assemble Bowl**:
   - Spread cauliflower rice in the bottom of a bowl.
   - Spoon shredded enchilada chicken and sauce generously over the rice.
   - Add black beans if using.
   - Top with a dollop of Greek yogurt, fresh cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
   - Add salsa and avocado if desired.

## Notes

- **Portion**: Single serving — 1 chicken breast + sauce + cauliflower rice.
- **Calories**: ~380 without avocado; ~430 with half an avocado.
- **Enchilada Sauce**: One standard can (10 oz) has roughly 1 cup of sauce. Use what you need; refrigerate or freeze the rest.
- **Cauliflower Rice**: Excellent low-calorie volume. A 2-cup portion is about 50-60 calories — far less than white rice at 400+ calories per cup.
- **Greek Yogurt vs. Sour Cream**: Greek yogurt has a very similar texture and tang with significantly more protein and less fat.
