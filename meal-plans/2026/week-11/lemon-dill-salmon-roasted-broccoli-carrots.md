# Lemon Dill Salmon with Roasted Broccoli and Carrots

A salmon filet covered in a lemon dill sauce, roasted alongside caramelized broccoli and carrots. Simple to prepare, bright and herby, deeply satisfying for very few calories. ~460 calories.

## Ingredients

### Salmon

- 1 salmon filet (about 5-6 oz), ~240 cal
- 1 lemon dill sauce packet or bag, ~140 cal

### Roasted Broccoli & Carrots

- 2 cups broccoli florets (about 0.5 head)
- 3 medium carrots, peeled and cut into 0.5-inch coins or diagonal slices
- 1 tsp olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 0.25 tsp red pepper flakes

### Finishing

- Fresh dill, roughly chopped (optional if you have extra from the sauce)
- Lemon wedge

**Prep Notes:**

- Pat the salmon filet dry before applying the sauce — moisture on the surface prevents the sauce from adhering and creates steam instead of a set glaze.
- Spread the sauce generously over the top and sides of the filet. If the sauce comes in a bag, you can bake the salmon directly in the bag; follow package directions.
- Broccoli florets should be similarly-sized so they roast evenly. Very large florets can be halved through the stem.
- Carrots take slightly longer than broccoli — put them on the pan first for 5 minutes before adding broccoli, or cut them thinner than the broccoli so they finish together.
- Roasting at high heat (425°F) caramelizes the broccoli tips and sweetens the carrots. Don't crowd the pan — spread in a single layer or the vegetables will steam.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Preheat**: Oven to 425°F. Line one or two baking sheets with parchment.

2. **Roast Carrots First**:
   - Toss carrot coins with 0.5 tsp olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Spread on baking sheet.
   - Roast 10 minutes before adding broccoli.

3. **Add Broccoli**:
   - Toss broccoli with remaining 0.5 tsp olive oil, garlic slices, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
   - Add to the baking sheet with carrots (or use a second sheet). Roast another 20-22 minutes until broccoli tips are crispy and dark brown and carrots are tender and caramelized.

4. **Cook Salmon**:
   - While vegetables roast, place salmon on a separate parchment-lined baking sheet (or follow bag instructions if baking in the bag).
   - Apply lemon dill sauce over the top of the filet. Season lightly with pepper.
   - Roast at 425°F for 12-15 minutes until the salmon flakes easily with a fork and the sauce is set. Thinner fillets may need only 10-12 minutes.

5. **Finish & Serve**:
   - Remove vegetables from oven. Squeeze lemon juice over broccoli and carrots. Add lemon zest if using.
   - Plate salmon alongside the roasted vegetables. Garnish with fresh dill and a lemon wedge.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~460 total (salmon ~240 + sauce ~140 + broccoli ~35 + carrots ~50 + oil ~35).
- **Timing**: Start the carrots at T-minus 30 minutes, add broccoli at T-minus 20, add salmon at T-minus 15. Everything finishes at roughly the same time.
