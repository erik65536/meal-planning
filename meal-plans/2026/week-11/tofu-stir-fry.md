# Tofu Stir Fry

Crispy pressed tofu tossed with frozen stir fry vegetables and a sesame teriyaki glaze. Same bold flavors as the pork stir fry, now built around plant protein. The key is getting the tofu genuinely crispy — seared, not steamed. ~480 calories.

## Ingredients

### Tofu

- 6 oz extra-firm tofu, pressed and cut into 0.75-inch cubes
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp ginger powder
- 1 tsp cornstarch (for crispiness — optional but highly recommended)

### Stir Fry

- 1 bag (12 oz) frozen stir fry vegetables (broccoli, snap peas, carrots, water chestnuts mix), ~180 cal
- 0.5 bottle sesame teriyaki sauce (about 3-4 tbsp), ~200 cal
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated (or 0.5 tsp ground)
- 0.5 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1 tsp rice vinegar or lime juice

### Finishing

- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 2 green onions, thinly sliced
- Red pepper flakes to taste

**Prep Notes:**

- Tofu must be pressed dry for at least 15 minutes before cooking — extra moisture is the single biggest reason tofu doesn't crisp. Wrap in a clean towel, press under a heavy pan, or use a tofu press. The drier the tofu, the better the crust.
- Toss pressed, cubed tofu with cornstarch before searing — a light dusting creates a much crispier exterior and helps the sauce adhere. Shake off any excess.
- Use a very hot pan and don't move the tofu for 2-3 minutes per side — patience and heat are the two requirements for crispy tofu.
- Cook the tofu and vegetables separately and combine at the end, just like the pork stir fry. Crowding causes steaming.
- Add sauce at the very end and let it caramelize for 60-90 seconds — this concentrates the flavor rather than leaving a watery glaze.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Press & Prep Tofu**:
   - Press tofu for at least 15 minutes until no more moisture releases. Cut into 0.75-inch cubes.
   - Toss cubes with salt, pepper, garlic powder, ginger powder, and cornstarch. Shake off any loose excess.

2. **Crisp the Tofu**:
   - Heat avocado oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat until just smoking.
   - Add tofu in a single layer — no crowding. Do not stir for 2-3 minutes until a golden crust forms on the bottom.
   - Flip carefully and cook another 2-3 minutes on each remaining side until golden on all sides. Remove and set aside.

3. **Cook Vegetables**:
   - In the same pan over high heat, add frozen stir fry vegetables directly from the bag.
   - Cook undisturbed 2-3 minutes, then stir and cook another 2-3 minutes until tender-crisp with char spots.
   - Add garlic, fresh ginger, and red pepper flakes; stir fry 1 minute.

4. **Combine & Glaze**:
   - Return tofu to the pan. Add sesame teriyaki sauce and rice vinegar. Toss gently to coat without breaking up the tofu.
   - Cook 60-90 seconds over high heat until sauce thickens and clings.

5. **Serve**:
   - Plate and top with sesame seeds, sliced green onions, and extra red pepper flakes.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~480 total (tofu ~100 + vegetables ~180 + teriyaki sauce ~200 + small oil/cornstarch ~20 + sesame seeds ~15).
