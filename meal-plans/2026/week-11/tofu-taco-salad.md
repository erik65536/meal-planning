# Tofu Taco Salad

Crumbled, taco-spiced tofu over a massive bed of romaine with salsa, pickled jalapeños, radishes, and a lime-cumin drizzle. Everything a taco bowl should be, without the shell and the cheese. ~265 calories.

## Ingredients

### Taco Tofu

- 6 oz extra-firm tofu, pressed and crumbled
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 1.5 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp soy sauce or tamari (low sodium)

### Salad Base

- 4 cups romaine lettuce, roughly chopped
- 0.5 cup salsa
- 4 radishes, thinly sliced into rounds
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Pickled jalapeño slices, to taste
- 1-2 tbsp frozen or canned corn, thawed (optional, ~15 cal)

### Lime-Cumin Drizzle

- 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt (0%)
- 1 tbsp lime juice (about 0.5 lime)
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp water (to thin)

### Finishing

- Fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
- Hot sauce (Cholula or Valentina)
- Extra lime wedge

**Prep Notes:**

- Press the tofu for at least 10 minutes before crumbling — a drier tofu crisps in the pan instead of steaming. Wrap in a clean towel and press under a heavy skillet, or use a tofu press.
- Crumble into uneven pieces ranging from pea-sized to almond-sized — the variety of sizes means some bits get crispy while others stay chewy.
- The soy sauce adds umami depth and helps the spices cling; add it in the last minute of cooking so it sizzles and caramelizes rather than creating steam.
- Radishes and cucumber add crunch and volume for nearly zero calories — load them up.
- Red onion soaked in cold water for 5 minutes loses much of its raw bite; drain before using.
- Salsa doubles as a dressing, which eliminates the need for a separate caloric dressing.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Press & Cook Tofu**:
   - Press tofu dry. Crumble into a bowl into rough, uneven pieces.
   - Toss with cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, cayenne, salt, and pepper.
   - Heat avocado oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add seasoned tofu crumbles in a single layer.
   - Cook undisturbed 3-4 minutes until the bottom develops a crust. Stir and cook another 3-4 minutes until browned and slightly crispy throughout.
   - Add soy sauce in the last minute. Stir and let it sizzle and coat. Remove from heat.

2. **Make Dressing**:
   - Whisk Greek yogurt, lime juice, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and water in a small bowl until smooth. Taste and adjust lime.

3. **Assemble**:
   - Spread romaine over a large bowl.
   - Add salsa, radishes, cherry tomatoes, corn (if using), and pickled jalapeños.
   - Spoon hot tofu crumbles over the top.
   - Drizzle lime-cumin yogurt over everything.
   - Top with fresh cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and hot sauce.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~265 total (tofu ~100 + salsa ~25 + vegetables ~60 + yogurt dressing ~25 + corn ~15 + small oil ~20).
