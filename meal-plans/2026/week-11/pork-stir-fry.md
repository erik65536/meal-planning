# Pork Stir Fry

Cubed pork chop seared hot and fast, then tossed with frozen stir fry vegetables and a bold sesame teriyaki glaze. Quick weeknight dinner with serious umami payoff. ~560 calories.

## Ingredients

### Pork

- 1 boneless pork chop (6 oz), cut into 0.75-inch cubes
- 1 tsp avocado oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp ginger powder

### Stir Fry

- 1 bag (12 oz) frozen stir fry vegetables (broccoli, snap peas, carrots, water chestnuts mix), ~180 cal
- 0.5 bottle sesame teriyaki sauce (about 3-4 tbsp), ~200 cal
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated (or 0.5 tsp ground)
- 0.5 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1 tsp rice vinegar or lime juice (to brighten the sauce)

### Finishing

- 1 tsp sesame seeds
- 2 green onions, thinly sliced
- Red pepper flakes to taste

**Prep Notes:**

- Cut the pork into uniform cubes so they sear evenly — too large and the inside undercooks before the outside browns.
- Pat pork cubes dry with paper towels before searing — moisture is the enemy of a good crust.
- Cook vegetables straight from frozen in a very hot pan; don't thaw first or they'll steam and turn soggy.
- Add sauce last and let it caramelize for 60-90 seconds — this concentrates the flavor and coats everything rather than making a watery pool.
- Work in two phases: sear the pork separately, then build the stir fry. Crowding the pan with both at once prevents browning.

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Sear Pork**:
   - Pat pork cubes dry, season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and ginger powder.
   - Heat avocado oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat until just smoking.
   - Add pork in a single layer. Do not stir for 90 seconds — let a crust form. Flip and cook another 60-90 seconds until cooked through (145°F internal) with browned edges. Remove and set aside.

2. **Cook Vegetables**:
   - In the same pan over high heat, add the frozen stir fry vegetables directly from the bag. Spread in a single layer.
   - Cook undisturbed 2-3 minutes, then stir and cook another 2-3 minutes until tender-crisp with some char spots. Add garlic, fresh ginger, and red pepper flakes; stir fry 1 minute.

3. **Combine & Glaze**:
   - Return pork to the pan. Add sesame teriyaki sauce and rice vinegar. Toss everything to coat.
   - Cook 60-90 seconds over high heat, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens and clings to the pork and vegetables.

4. **Serve**:
   - Plate and top with sesame seeds, sliced green onions, and extra red pepper flakes.

## Notes

- **Calories**: ~560 total (pork ~180 + vegetables ~180 + teriyaki sauce ~200).
