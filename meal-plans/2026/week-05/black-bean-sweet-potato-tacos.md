# Black Bean & Sweet Potato Tacos with Cilantro Lime Slaw

## Ingredients

### Taco Filling

- 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced into 0.5-inch cubes
- 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- 0.5 tsp garlic powder
- 0.25 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
- Juice of 1 lime

### Cilantro Lime Slaw (Side & Topping)

- 3 cups green cabbage, shredded
- 1 cup carrots, shredded or julienned
- 0.25 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 0.5 tsp cumin
- 1 cup plain low-fat Greek yogurt

### Additional Taco Toppings

- Pickled red onions
- Grated cheese
- Hot sauce or salsa verde

### Tortillas

- 8-10 low-carb / low-calorie tortillas

**Prep Notes:**

- Sweet potatoes can be roasted in the oven or cooked in a skillet
- The cilantro lime slaw doubles as a fresh, crunchy, low-calorie side dish and a taco topping

## Cooking Instructions

1. **Prepare Cilantro Lime Slaw**:
   - In a medium bowl, combine shredded cabbage, carrots, and cilantro
   - Whisk together lime juice, olive oil, cumin, and yogurt in a small bowl
   - Toss with cabbage mixture and refrigerate until serving

2. **Cook Sweet Potatoes**:
   - Preheat oven to 400°F
   - Toss diced sweet potatoes with olive oil, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, cayenne, and salt
   - Spread in single layer on a lined baking sheet
   - Roast 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway, until tender and lightly caramelized

3. **Warm Black Beans**:
   - In a small saucepan, warm black beans over medium heat with a splash of water
   - Season with a pinch of cumin and salt
   - Lightly mash about a third of the beans for a creamier texture

4. **Assemble Tacos**:
   - Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over a gas flame
   - Layer each tortilla with black beans, roasted sweet potatoes, pickled red onions, and cilantro lime slaw
   - Top with cheese, fresh cilantro, and hot sauce or salsa verde
   - Squeeze extra lime over the top

5. **Serve**:
   - Plate tacos with remaining cilantro lime slaw on the side

## Notes

- Black beans and sweet potatoes together provide complete protein, fiber, and complex carbs
- Pickled red onions add bright acidity with almost no calories — make extra to keep in the fridge
- Cilantro lime slaw is very low calorie and adds crunch, freshness, and volume
- Greek yogurt is a high-protein, lower-calorie substitute for sour cream
- Sweet potatoes provide beta-carotene and antioxidants
- This is a filling, satisfying vegetarian meal
