# Shopping List — Week 20

**Meals this week:**
- Spaghetti and Meatballs with Shirataki Noodles
- Bistro Burger Board — Pâté & Egg-Latke
- Cabbage Steak Pizza with Chicken

---

## Proteins

- **1 lb ground beef, 80/20** - _Spaghetti & Meatballs (8 oz meatballs), Bistro Burger Board (8 oz patties)_
- **1 boneless skinless chicken breast (~6 oz)** - _Cabbage Steak Pizza_
- **1 small container chicken liver pâté (or duck pâté)** - _Bistro Burger Board (2 tbsp; Braunschweiger is a widely available alternative)_

## Canned & Dry Goods

- **2 cans (14.5 oz) whole peeled tomatoes** - _Spaghetti & Meatballs (sauce), Cabbage Steak Pizza (sauce)_
- **1 small can or tube tomato paste** - _Spaghetti & Meatballs (1 tbsp), Cabbage Steak Pizza (1 tbsp)_
- **1 package (7-8 oz) shirataki spaghetti noodles** - _Spaghetti & Meatballs_
- **2 slices sandwich bread (white or whole wheat)** - _Spaghetti & Meatballs (meatball panade)_
- **1 small jar sun-dried tomatoes, oil-packed** - _Cabbage Steak Pizza (1 tbsp)_
- **1 jar pickled jalapeño slices** - _Bistro Burger Board (Egg-Latke Burger)_
- **1 jar banana pepper rings** - _Cabbage Steak Pizza_
- **[Optional] 1 small can or jar black olives** - _Cabbage Steak Pizza_
- **[Optional] 1 jar roasted red peppers** - _Cabbage Steak Pizza (easy alternative to roasting fresh; skip if using the fresh red bell pepper below)_

- **1 bunch fresh parsley** - _Spaghetti & Meatballs (0.25 cup meatballs, garnish)_
- **1 bunch fresh basil** - _Spaghetti & Meatballs (sauce, garnish), Cabbage Steak Pizza (garnish)_
- **1 small bunch fresh chives (or use green onion tops)** - _Bistro Burger Board (Egg-Latke Burger yogurt dollop, 1 tsp)_
- **6 oz fingerling potatoes** - _Bistro Burger Board (side)_
- **1 medium russet potato (~6 oz)** - _Bistro Burger Board (latke)_
- **1 medium red bell pepper** - _Bistro Burger Board (side, 0.5), Cabbage Steak Pizza (roasted topping, 0.5)_
- **1 medium orange or yellow bell pepper** - _Bistro Burger Board (side)_
- **2 oz cremini or white mushrooms** - _Cabbage Steak Pizza_
- **1 head cauliflower** - _Staple_
- **1 bag baby carrots** - _Staple_
