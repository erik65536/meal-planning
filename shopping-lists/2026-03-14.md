# Shopping List — Week 11 (2026-03-13)

**This week's meals:**

- Pork Stir Fry
- Red Lentil Dal with Roasted Cauliflower and Carrots _(make double, eat 2 days)_
- Tofu Taco Salad
- Lemon Dill Salmon with Roasted Broccoli and Carrots
- Chicken Noodle Soup
- Tofu Stir Fry

---

## Proteins

- **1 boneless pork chop (6 oz)** - _Pork Stir Fry_
- **1 salmon filet (5-6 oz)** - _Lemon Dill Salmon_
- **1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (6 oz)** - _Chicken Noodle Soup_
- **1 block (14-16 oz) extra-firm tofu** - _Tofu Taco Salad (6 oz), Tofu Stir Fry (6 oz)_

## Canned & Dry Goods

- **0.75 cup red lentils** - _Lentil Dal_
- **1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes** - _Lentil Dal_
- **1 package (7-8 oz) miracle noodles (shirataki)** - _Chicken Noodle Soup_
- **1 jar pickled jalapeños** - _Tofu Taco Salad_
- **1 jar salsa (at least 0.5 cup)** - _Tofu Taco Salad_

## Produce — Fresh Vegetables

- **9 medium carrots** - _Lentil Dal roasted side (4), Lemon Dill Salmon (3), Chicken Noodle Soup (2)_
- **1 head cauliflower** - _Lentil Dal roasted side_
- **1 head broccoli (about 2 cups florets)** - _Lemon Dill Salmon_
- **1 medium yellow onion** - _Lentil Dal_
- **1 head garlic** - _Pork Stir Fry (2 cloves), Lentil Dal (4), Lemon Dill Salmon (3), Chicken Noodle Soup (3), Tofu Stir Fry (2)_
- **2-inch knob fresh ginger** - _Pork Stir Fry (1 tsp), Lentil Dal (1 tbsp), Tofu Stir Fry (1 tsp)_
- **3 stalks celery** - _Chicken Noodle Soup_
- **1 head romaine lettuce** - _Tofu Taco Salad (4 cups)_
- **1 cup cherry tomatoes** - _Tofu Taco Salad_
- **1 bunch radishes (4 needed)** - _Tofu Taco Salad_
- **1 bunch green onions** - _Pork Stir Fry (2), Tofu Stir Fry (2)_
- **1 bunch fresh cilantro** - _Lentil Dal (topping), Tofu Taco Salad_
- **1 small bunch fresh dill** - _Chicken Noodle Soup (1 tbsp), Lemon Dill Salmon (finishing)_

## Produce — Fresh Fruit

- **2 lemons** - _Lentil Dal (finishing + topping), Chicken Noodle Soup (finishing), Lemon Dill Salmon (serving)_
- **2 limes** - _Tofu Taco Salad (0.5 lime + extra wedge), Pork Stir Fry or Tofu Stir Fry (if using lime over rice vinegar)_

## Frozen

- **2 bags (12 oz each) frozen stir fry vegetables** - _Pork Stir Fry, Tofu Stir Fry_
- **Small bag frozen corn (optional, ~15 cal addition)** - _Tofu Taco Salad (1-2 tbsp)_

## Dairy & Refrigerated

- **1 container (6 oz) plain Greek yogurt, 0%** - _Lentil Dal topping (4 tbsp total), Tofu Taco Salad (2 tbsp)_

## Oils, Vinegars & Condiments

- **1 bottle sesame teriyaki sauce** - _Pork Stir Fry (0.5 bottle), Tofu Stir Fry (0.5 bottle)_
- **1 lemon dill sauce packet or bag** - _Lemon Dill Salmon_
- **Rice vinegar** - _Pork Stir Fry (1 tsp), Tofu Stir Fry (1 tsp)_
- **Low-sodium soy sauce or tamari** - _Tofu Taco Salad (1 tsp)_
- **Hot sauce (Cholula or Valentina)** - _Tofu Taco Salad_

- **Mustard seeds** - _Lentil Dal (1 tsp)_
- **Curry powder** - _Lentil Dal (2 tsp dal + 1 tsp roasting)_
- **Garam masala** - _Lentil Dal (1 tsp)_
