## Notes

- **Black olives**: One small can (~3.8 oz drained) covers both the Chicken Wraps (0.25 cup) and the Mediterranean Skillet (0.25 cup).
- **Greek yogurt**: One 16 oz container covers all three uses this week — Chicken Wraps, Shakshuka, and Enchilada Bowl.
- **Garlic**: One full head will cover all 6 recipes that use garlic.
- **Cherry tomatoes**: 1 pint covers Salmon, Chicken Wraps, and Skillet.
- **Enchilada sauce**: One 10 oz can is enough for a single serving — don't feel pressure to buy the large can.
- **Eggs**: One dozen covers both egg meals (4 eggs each) with extras.
- **Portion control reminder**: Buy the smallest package size available whenever possible. You don't have to finish the whole package in one meal.
